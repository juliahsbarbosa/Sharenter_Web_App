About Us

Sharenter was founded because of the difficulty in finding a place to live in Dublin. The platform helps people find and rent rooms in shared houses. We offer a wide range of rooms to choose from, makes it easier for people to find and rent rooms in shared houses. and to find the perfect housemate.

We believe that everyone should have access to affordable, safe, and comfortable housing. We are committed to providing the best possible experience for our users, and we hope that you will choose Sharenter for your next house share.

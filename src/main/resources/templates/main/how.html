Sharenter - FAQ

Renter

1. Register/Login: Create an account and login to the app.

2. Browse Listings: Look through listings of houseshares in your area.

3. Send Message: Once you've found a listing you're interested in, send the owner a message.

Sharer

1. Register or Login: You can register for free as a sharer. After you login, you will be able to add your house .

2. Add your house vancancy: Once you are logged in, click on "Add your house vancancy". Fill out the form with as many details as possible.

3. Receive messages: After you submit your form, you will start receiving messages from potential sharers.
